About Us

Welcome to the website of Urmia University, Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Software Engineering Department.
This website is designed to provide information and tools related to algorithms, specifically in the fields of search engines and web mining.

About the Algorithms Section and the Website

This section allows you to become familiar with various algorithms and concepts associated with them. The algorithms covered here include Decision Trees, KNN, SVM, and K-Means. Through this section, you can not only learn about these algorithms but also understand how to apply them in different contexts.

Implemented by Students

This website was designed and implemented by Hadiseh Khorasani, a graduate student of Urmia University, under the supervision of Dr. Mir Saman Tajbakhsh. This project aims to increase students' knowledge and skills in the field of search engines and web mining.

Additional Information

In this website, besides providing information about algorithms, you can find answers to your questions and clarifications regarding this field. Additionally, you can explore practical implementations of algorithms and related content.

Thank you for using this website. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions or need further information.
